About Us


We are all about Design

Liseli J Branding House is a limited company that specialises in branding. We aim to provide end-to-end high-quality branding to SMEs and corporates. Building a timeless brand, takes time, a creative mind and consistency. As Liseli J Branding House, we build such brands for all our clients. Our core services are Company Branding, Product Branding, Website Design, Build & Hosting, Social Media Content Creation & Management , Photography and Digital Art Prints.

Our Mission

To produce creative branding of the best quality that will appeal to Zambia, Africa and the world.

Our Values
